Exercise 1.2 — Trust Is Earned, Not Claimed

Credibility is when others feel safe repeating what you say about yourself.
5
Why AWS would confidently introduce you. Why risk feels low. Why failure feels unlikely.
5
Why a public-sector or enterprise SAP buyer would listen. Why the problem feels familiar. Why timing and urgency are real.
5
What evidence shows this has worked before or can be delivered reliably.
Enter a trust statement and score it. The app will place the card where trust is currently being earned strongest.
Facilitator instruction:
Ask participants to convert broad claims into evidence-backed statements that open a targeted AWS engagement. The strongest statements are forward-looking, buyer-relevant, low-risk, and supported by delivery proof.
